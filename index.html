About Me

I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Texas at Austin, where my fascination with graphics and games fuels my academic journey. To me, games represent a unique blend of artistic creativity and innovative technology, where visuals, audio, storytelling, and interesting mechanics intertwine to create powerful experiences for players. I believe that the core of a great game lies in its ability to transport players to new worlds through stunning visuals combined with responsive gameplay.

To that end, I strive to create captivating visuals that align with the particular style of a game, whether it be cartoonish, artistic, or realistic, all while optimizing performance to ensure a seamless experience. You can view my progress on my path to achieve these goals in my project highlights and artistic portfolio below.

Currently, I am pursuing a Computer Science degree at UT with a planned graduation date of December 2023. Aside from graphics and art, I also have an interest in video games and tabletop, with my favorites currently being Don't Starve, League of Legends, Minecraft, and Ars Magica. I also enjoy outdoor activities (when the weather isn't too hot) such as hiking, rock climbing, and camping.
